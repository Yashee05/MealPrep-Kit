RAJMA CHAWAL

10-15 min

Needed

Rs. 250/serving

Ingredients

  • Pre-soaked and boiled kidney beans (rajma)
  • Ready-made tomato-onion gravy (pureed and pre-cooked)
  • Pre-mixed spice blend (cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder)
  • Pre-boiled rice
  • Pre-chopped coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add the pre-made tomato-onion gravy.
  2. Add the spice mix and stir well.
  3. Add the boiled kidney beans and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Garnish with coriander and serve with pre-boiled rice.
