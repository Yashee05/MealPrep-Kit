Frequently Asked Questions

What is Meal Prep? ▼
Meal Prep is a service that provides fresh, healthy, and delicious meal kits delivered straight to your door. We offer a variety of cuisines for every taste and dietary preference.
How do I order? ▼
You can browse our menu, select your meals, and add them to your cart. After checkout, your meals will be delivered to your doorstep at your convenience.
Are your meals customizable? ▼
Yes! You can customize your meals to fit your dietary preferences and restrictions. Simply choose from a variety of options when ordering.
What if I have allergies? ▼
We take allergies seriously. During checkout, you can specify any food allergies, and we will provide alternative meal options to ensure your safety.
How do I contact customer support? ▼
You can reach our customer support team through our contact page or by emailing us directly at support@mealprep.com.
