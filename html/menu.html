RAJMA CHAWAL

Classic North Indian dish featuring creamy kidney beans cooked in a rich, spiced tomato gravy, served with steamed rice. It’s a comforting and nutritious meal, perfect for a hearty and wholesome experience.

BUTTER PANEER

Rich and creamy dish made with soft paneer cubes simmered in a luscious tomato-based gravy infused with butter and aromatic spices. It pairs perfectly with naan or rice for a delicious and indulgent meal.

DAL TADKA

Dal Tadka is a flavorful Indian lentil dish made with cooked yellow lentils tempered with aromatic spices, garlic, and ghee. It’s a comforting and nutritious meal, best enjoyed with steamed rice or roti.

Italian pasta dish made with garlic sautéed in olive oil, red chili flakes, and parsley. It’s a light and aromatic dish that highlights the richness of olive oil and the heat of chili.
