Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

5-10 min

Needed

Rs. 350/serving

Ingredients

  • Pre-cooked spaghetti
  • Pre-minced garlic and olive oil
  • Red chili flakes
  • Pre-chopped parsley
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté pre-minced garlic until golden.
  2. Add red chili flakes and stir for a few seconds.
  3. Toss in pre-cooked spaghetti and mix well.
  4. Garnish with parsley and Parmesan (if included), then serve hot.
