Cheese Quesadillas

10-15 min

Rs. 350/serving

Ingredients

  • Pre-grated cheese (cheddar & mozzarella)
  • Pre-chopped bell peppers and mushrooms
  • Soft tortillas
  • Pre-made salsa and sour cream

Instructions

  1. Place cheese and vegetables on a tortilla and fold it in half.
  2. Cook on a pan until golden brown and cheese melts.
  3. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
