BUTTER PANEER

5-10 min

Rs. 350/serving

Ingredients

  • Pre-cut paneer cubes
  • Ready-made makhani gravy (tomato-cashew base)
  • Pre-mixed spice blend (garam masala, kasuri methi, chili powder)
  • Pre-boiled cream or butter for finishing
  • Pre-chopped coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the ready-made makhani gravy in a pan.
  2. Add the pre-cut paneer cubes and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Stir in cream/butter and cook for another minute.
  4. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
