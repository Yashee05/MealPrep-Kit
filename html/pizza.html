Margherita Pizza

5-10 min

Rs. 460/serving

Ingredients

  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Ready-made tomato sauce
  • Pre-sliced fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Spread tomato sauce over the pre-made pizza dough.
  2. Arrange mozzarella slices on top.
  3. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 10-12 minutes until the cheese melts.
  4. Garnish with fresh basil and drizzle olive oil before serving.
