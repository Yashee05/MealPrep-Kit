Mexican Rice Bowl

10-15 min

Needed

Rs. 250/serving

Ingredients

  • Pre-cooked Mexican rice
  • Pre-cooked black beans
  • Pre-chopped bell peppers, corn, and onions
  • Pre-made salsa and guacamole

Instructions

  1. Heat the Mexican rice and beans together.
  2. Add pre-chopped vegetables and mix well.
  3. Serve with salsa and guacamole.
